## 1. STATEMENT OF CASH FLOWS (IAS 7) - Direct Method

### Format Structure:
```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                            [COMPANY NAME]
                       STATEMENT OF CASH FLOWS
                   For the year ended [DATE]
                           Direct Method
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                                        [Year]    [Prior Year]
                                                         $'000         $'000

OPERATING ACTIVITIES
Cash receipts from customers                            X,XXX         X,XXX
Cash paid to suppliers                                 (X,XXX)       (X,XXX)
Cash paid to employees                                 (X,XXX)       (X,XXX)
                                                       ───────       ───────
Cash generated from operations                          X,XXX         X,XXX
Interest paid                                            (XXX)         (XXX)
Income tax paid                                          (XXX)         (XXX)
                                                       ───────       ───────
Net cash from operating activities                      X,XXX         X,XXX
                                                       ═══════       ═══════

INVESTING ACTIVITIES
Purchase of property, plant and equipment                (XXX)         (XXX)
Proceeds from sale of equipment                           XXX           XXX
Purchase of investments                                  (XXX)         (XXX)
Proceeds from sale of investments                         XXX           XXX
Interest received                                          XX            XX
Dividends received                                         XX            XX
                                                       ───────       ───────
Net cash used in investing activities                    (XXX)         (XXX)
                                                       ═══════       ═══════

FINANCING ACTIVITIES
Proceeds from issuance of share capital                   XXX           XXX
Proceeds from long-term borrowings                        XXX           XXX
Repayment of borrowings                                  (XXX)         (XXX)
Payment of lease liabilities                              (XX)          (XX)
Dividends paid                                           (XXX)         (XXX)
                                                       ───────       ───────
Net cash from financing activities                        XXX           XXX
                                                       ═══════       ═══════

NET INCREASE/(DECREASE) IN CASH                         X,XXX         X,XXX

Cash and cash equivalents at beginning of year          X,XXX         X,XXX
                                                       ───────       ───────
Cash and cash equivalents at end of year               XX,XXX        XX,XXX
                                                       ═══════       ═══════
```

### Key Classification Rules (IAS 7):

**Operating Activities:**
- Principal revenue-producing activities
- Cash receipts from sale of goods/services
- Cash payments to suppliers and employees
- Interest paid (can be operating or financing)
- Tax payments

**Investing Activities:**
- Acquisition and disposal of long-term assets
- Purchase/sale of property, plant & equipment
- Purchase/sale of investments
- Interest received (can be operating or investing)
- Dividends received (can be operating or investing)

**Financing Activities:**
- Changes in size/composition of equity and borrowings
- Proceeds from issuing shares
- Proceeds from issuing bonds/borrowings
- Repayment of borrowings
- Payment of lease liabilities
- Dividends paid (can be financing or operating)

```
                          ──────    ──────     ──────      ─────       ───────      ──────     ───────

  with owners                XXX       XXX       (XXX)        --          (XXX)         XX        (XXX)
                          ──────    ──────     ──────      ─────       ───────      ──────     ───────

                          ══════    ══════     ══════      ═════       ═══════      ══════     ═══════
```

### Equity Components Explained:

1. **Share Capital**: Issued ordinary shares at par value
2. **Share Premium**: Amount received above par value
3. **Revaluation Reserve**: Gains from revaluing property (IAS 16)
4. **Foreign Currency Translation Reserve**: Gains/losses from translating foreign operations (IAS 21)
5. **Retained Earnings**: Accumulated profits retained in the business
6. **Other Reserves**: 
   - Actuarial gains/losses reserve (IAS 19)
   - Share-based payment reserve (IFRS 2)
   - Cash flow hedge reserve (IFRS 9)
   - Fair value reserve (IFRS 9)

## 6. PRESENTATION REQUIREMENTS CHECKLIST

### Cash Flow Statement (IAS 7):
- [ ] Separate sections for Operating, Investing, and Financing
- [ ] Clear subtotals for each section
- [ ] Net increase/decrease in cash clearly shown
- [ ] Opening and closing cash balances
- [ ] Comparative period(s) presented
- [ ] Classification consistent year-over-year
- [ ] Non-cash transactions disclosed in notes
- [ ] Significant transactions explained in notes
- [ ] Reconciliation of cash and cash equivalents to balance sheet
- [ ] Method used (direct or indirect) clearly stated

### Statement of Changes in Equity (IAS 1):
- [ ] All components of equity shown as separate columns
- [ ] Opening balance for each component
- [ ] Changes classified as:
  - [ ] Profit or loss
  - [ ] Other comprehensive income
  - [ ] Transactions with owners
- [ ] Closing balance for each component
- [ ] Total equity column showing sum of all components
- [ ] Comparative period presented
- [ ] Notes explaining nature of reserves
- [ ] Dividends per share disclosed (if applicable)
- [ ] Reconciliation to retained earnings

## 7. FORMATTING STANDARDS FOR PROFESSIONAL PRESENTATION

### Number Formatting:
```
Positive numbers:      12,345,678
Negative numbers:     (12,345,678)  or  -12,345,678
Zero amounts:               --      or       -
Subtotals:            ──────────
Grand totals:         ══════════
```

### Indentation Levels:
```
Level 0: Main Section Headers (OPERATING ACTIVITIES)
Level 1:   Main line items (Cash receipts from customers)
Level 2:     Sub-items (Adjustments for:)
Level 3:       Detail items (Depreciation and amortization)
Level 4:         Further detail if needed
```

### Font and Styling:
- Section headers: **BOLD UPPERCASE**
- Subtotals: **Bold**
- Line items: Regular
- Notes: Italic or smaller font

### Page Layout:
- Portrait orientation for standard reports
- Landscape orientation for multi-period comparatives
- A4 or Letter size
- Margins: 2.5cm (1 inch) all sides
- Header: Company name, report title, period
- Footer: Page numbers, preparation date

## 8. DIGITAL EXPORT FORMATS

### PDF Requirements:
- Searchable text (not scanned images)
- Bookmarks for major sections
- Proper metadata (title, author, date)
- Security: Allow printing and copying
- Compliance: PDF/A-3 for archival

### Excel Requirements:
- Separate worksheets for each statement
- Formulas preserved (not values only)
- Print area defined
- Page breaks set appropriately
- Headers/footers configured
- No hidden rows/columns in final version

### CSV Requirements:
- UTF-8 encoding
- Comma delimited
- Quotes around text fields
- Consistent date format (ISO 8601)
- Header row included

---

## 9. AUDIT TRAIL REQUIREMENTS

Every report must have:
1. **Generation timestamp**: Date and time report was created
2. **Parameters used**: Date range, branches, filters applied
3. **Data source**: Database version or backup used
4. **User who generated**: Username or ID
5. **Version number**: Report template version
6. **Hash or checksum**: For tamper detection (optional but recommended)

Example footer:
```
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Generated: 17 February 2026 14:35:22 UTC
Period: 01 January 2025 to 31 December 2025
Branches: Head Office, Branch A, Branch B
Generated by: john.doe@company.com
Template Version: 2.3.1
Report ID: CF-2025-12-001
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
```
